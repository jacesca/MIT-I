If anyone who is new to probability and inferential stats wants a fun resource, this book will make you laugh: 

Antifragile by Nassim Nicholas Taleb 
(author of NY times best seller Black Swan). 

He covers how certain statisticians, policy makers, and scientists use probability calculations incorrectly.  It is engaging and educational and will help you grasp concepts
